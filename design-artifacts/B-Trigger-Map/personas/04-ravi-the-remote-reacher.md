## Profile Summary

Ravi is an experienced technical candidate applying to remote/global opportunities. His process spans platforms, time zones, and varied recruiting patterns, so consistency and timeline reliability are critical.

---

## Current Situation

**The Daily Struggle:**
- Asynchronous communication windows are easy to miss
- Opportunity data spreads across multiple source channels
- Longer hiring cycles create process drift and context loss

**Skills & Tools:**
- Strong technical fluency and self-management
- Uses mixed tools that do not preserve a consistent pipeline view
- Values complete history and reliable reminders

---

## Driving Forces

### Positive Drivers

1. Manage global opportunities in one consistent system
2. Preserve complete status and communication history
3. Stay responsive despite asynchronous recruiter timelines

### Negative Drivers

1. Missing critical windows due to time-zone delays
2. Fragmented source tracking and duplicate effort
3. Losing strategic visibility over long timelines

---

## Product Promises for Ravi

- Centralized lifecycle view across all sources
- Reliable reminders for overdue or stalled movement
- Stable audit trail for decision quality over time

---

## Success Indicators

- Fewer missed async follow-up windows
- Better continuity across long-running applications
- Improved comparison and prioritization quality
