# Priya the Pipeline Optimizer - Primary Persona

> PRIMARY target - throughput-focused professional who benefits most from guided workflow and reminders.

**Priority:** PRIMARY
**Role in Flywheel:** Drives the core success metric (`applications/week +25%`) through consistent high-volume use.
**Created:** 2026-03-26

---

## Profile Summary

Priya is a mid-career technical job seeker applying across multiple sources simultaneously. She is highly motivated and organized in intent, but her current toolset fragments her execution. She needs a system that preserves momentum, clarifies next actions, and reduces tracking overhead.

---

## Current Situation

**The Daily Struggle:**
- Juggling opportunities across tabs, notes, and spreadsheets
- Forgetting follow-ups when context switches between applications
- Spending too much time cleaning data instead of progressing applications

**Skills & Tools:**
- Strong technical literacy
- Uses spreadsheets/notes and ad hoc reminders
- Comfortable with structured workflows when they are fast

---

## Driving Forces

### Positive Drivers

1. Increase weekly application output without chaos
2. Always know the next best action per application
3. Keep momentum with low-friction updates

### Negative Drivers

1. Losing track across tools
2. Missing follow-ups due to manual gaps
3. Feeling busy but not progressing outcomes

---

## Product Promises for Priya

- Guided stage transitions with clear next-action prompts
- One canonical status model from `identified` to outcome
- Email nudges when action is required or progress stalls

---

## Success Indicators

- More applications submitted per week
- Fewer stalled opportunities
- Reduced time from `identified` to `submitted`
