# Ethan the Efficient Explorer - Secondary Persona

> SECONDARY target - high-volume applicant who prioritizes speed and low friction.

**Priority:** SECONDARY
**Role in Flywheel:** Expands adoption among broad-search users and validates capture efficiency.
**Created:** 2026-03-26

---

## Profile Summary

Ethan is an early-to-mid career technical candidate applying broadly. He explores many opportunities quickly and needs a tracking loop that is lightweight enough to keep up with his pace.

---

## Current Situation

**The Daily Struggle:**
- High volume creates inconsistent tracking habits
- Application records become incomplete under time pressure
- Reminder fatigue leads to ignored follow-ups

**Skills & Tools:**
- Comfortable with digital tools but avoids heavy setup
- Often starts in job boards and copies data manually
- Values speed over detailed metadata in early stages

---

## Driving Forces

### Positive Drivers

1. Capture opportunities quickly from multiple sources
2. Standardize progress tracking with minimal overhead
3. See visible movement that reinforces momentum

### Negative Drivers

1. Entry friction causing abandoned tracking
2. Inconsistent stage naming and process drift
3. Notification noise that feels like spam

---

## Product Promises for Ethan

- Fast manual + URL capture with minimal required fields
- Frictionless stage updates
- Notification controls that keep reminders useful

---

## Success Indicators

- Higher percentage of opportunities entered same-day
- Fewer incomplete records
- Better continuity from discovery to submission
