## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Bring Clara directly from a status-change email into the exact application context she needs to act on.

**Entry Context:** Clara clicks a status-change email link and lands in the app with immediate context for the updated application.

**Exit Action:** Clara opens `Application Detail` to review current stage and upcoming phase path.

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Validate secure one-click/deep-link session handling
- Show alert context for what changed
- Provide direct CTA to view application details

**Mental State:**
- **Hope:** The system provides clear next steps.
