## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Land Clara from a status-change email directly into the relevant application context, with immediate visibility of what changed and a clear CTA to act.

**User Situation:** Clara clicked a status-change notification email and arrived in the app expecting immediate context about the updated application.

**Success Criteria:** Clara instantly sees what changed and has a single clear action to take next.

**Entry Points:**
- Status-change email link (deep link) — only entry path

**Exit Points:**
- View application detail CTA → Application Detail (2.2)

## Mental State

- **Trigger:** Received a status-change notification and clicked through to act on it
- **Hope:** Lands immediately in the right place — sees what changed and knows what to do next
- **Worry:** The link feels broken, confusing, or dumps her on a generic page with no context about why she's there

---

## Desired Outcome

**User Goal:** Instantly see what changed and have a single clear action to take next.

**Business Goal:** Convert the email click into an in-app action — keep Clara in the workflow rather than bouncing back to email.
