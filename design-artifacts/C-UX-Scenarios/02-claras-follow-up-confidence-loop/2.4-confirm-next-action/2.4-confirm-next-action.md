## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Let Clara commit to the recommended action — confirm it's done or schedule a reminder — and close the loop with a clear sense of what happens next.

**User Situation:** Clara has received the guided recommendation and chosen to act — either immediately or by scheduling a reminder.

**Success Criteria:** Clara leaves with total confidence — the action is logged, a reminder is set if needed, and she knows what comes next in the process.

**Entry Points:**
- "Confirm & continue" CTA from Next-Step Guidance (2.3) — primary path
- "Schedule reminder" path from Next-Step Guidance (2.3) — alternate entry

**Exit Points:**
- Back to Application Detail (2.2) — to review the updated record
- Back to Dashboard (1.2) — return to pipeline

## Mental State

- **Trigger:** Chose to act on the recommendation — either do it now or schedule it
- **Hope:** Feels the loop close — confirmation is clear, she knows what to expect next and when
- **Worry:** It's not clear whether her action was actually recorded, or she's unsure what "confirmed" means for her pipeline

---

## Desired Outcome

**User Goal:** Leave with total confidence — the action is logged, a reminder is set if needed, and she knows what comes next in the process.

**Business Goal:** Record the committed action against the application record, advancing it in the pipeline and reducing stalled follow-through.

## Variants
