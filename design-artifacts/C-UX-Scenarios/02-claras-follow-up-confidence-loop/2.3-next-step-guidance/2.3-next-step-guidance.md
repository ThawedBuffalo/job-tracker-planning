## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Present Clara with a single, clearly reasoned recommended action for her current application stage — what to do, why it matters now, and how to schedule or complete it.

**User Situation:** Clara has reviewed the application detail and is ready to receive specific direction on what to do next.

**Success Criteria:** Clara walks away knowing exactly what to do next, why it matters, and when to do it — no ambiguity.

**Entry Points:**
- "See recommended next step" CTA from Application Detail (2.2) — primary path
- Automatic routing after email deep link resolves (system detects a pending action)

**Exit Points:**
- Confirm / schedule action CTA → Confirm Next Action (2.4)
- Back → Application Detail (2.2)

## Mental State

- **Trigger:** Clicked through from application detail to get specific direction
- **Hope:** Gets a clear, confident recommendation — one thing to do, not a list of options
- **Worry:** Guidance is vague or feels generic — "send a follow-up email" with no further help doesn't build confidence

---

## Desired Outcome

**User Goal:** Walk away knowing exactly what to do next, why it matters, and when to do it — no ambiguity.

**Business Goal:** Move Clara from passive awareness to committed action, reducing stalled applications in the pipeline.
