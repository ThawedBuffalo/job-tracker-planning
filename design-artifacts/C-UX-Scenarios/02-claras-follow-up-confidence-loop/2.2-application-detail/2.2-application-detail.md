## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Show Clara the full context of a specific application — current status, stage history, and a clear visual path of what comes next — so she can act with confidence.

**User Situation:** Clara has arrived from an email alert or dashboard tap, focused on understanding a specific application's current state.

**Success Criteria:** Clara understands the current stage, sees the upcoming path, and identifies the next recommended action without scrolling or hunting.

**Entry Points:**
- Email Link Entry (2.1) — primary path in this scenario
- Dashboard application list — direct tap on any existing application
- Browser back from Next-Step Guidance (2.3) — returning to review context

**Exit Points:**
- Next-step guidance CTA → Next-Step Guidance (2.3)
- Back → Dashboard (1.2)

## Mental State

- **Trigger:** Just arrived from a status-change alert or checking in on a specific role
- **Hope:** Sees the full picture at a glance — current stage, what's been done, what's coming next
- **Worry:** Detail view is too raw or buried — can't quickly read where she stands or what she should do

---

## Desired Outcome

**User Goal:** Understand the current stage, see the upcoming path, and identify the next recommended action — all without scrolling or hunting.

**Business Goal:** Give Clara enough clarity to proceed to Next-Step Guidance rather than abandoning the workflow.
