## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Let Ravi start a unified application entry from any external job site by pasting a job URL into the app — the first step toward consolidating fragmented sources into one workflow.

**User Situation:** Ravi is switching between multiple job sources and wants to move one promising opportunity into his central workflow without repetitive manual entry.

**Success Criteria:** Ravi pastes the URL, the system begins extraction, and he advances to review the parsed data.

**Entry Points:**
- Add application CTA from Dashboard (1.2) — primary path
- FAB / nav shortcut — quick access while browsing external sites

**Exit Points:**
- Parse initiates → Parsed Data Review (4.2)
- Parse fails → remains on page with manual entry fallback (interaction state)

## Mental State

- **Trigger:** Found a promising role on another site, wants it in the system immediately
- **Hope:** Paste, parse, review — done. No re-typing what the job posting already says
- **Worry:** App feels clunky or doesn't recognize the source URL — fragmentation continues

---

## Desired Outcome

**User Goal:** Paste the external URL and let the system do the heavy lifting — move to review without manual entry.

**Business Goal:** Capture externally discovered roles into the pipeline and reduce the friction that causes users to abandon tracking cross-site opportunities.
